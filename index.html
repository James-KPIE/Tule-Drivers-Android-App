OUR SERVICES

Because we understand that each organisation is unique, and, therefore has unique needs and requirements, we work collaboratively with our clients, deploying the latest business thinking to help them achieve a quantum leap in performance.

Our services include but not limited to:

Leadership & People Development

Breakthrough Leadership Programme: our flagship programme. If you desire to achieve breakthrough results for your organisation, then the Breakthrough Leadership Programme is for you. This programme was designed for C-Suite/senior executives—those responsible for setting organisational direction and responsible for bottom line impact to help them transform their organisations and achieve breakthrough results. Participants learn to use our Breakthrough Results Model to achieve breakthrough business results.

Duration: Three to Five Days

Leading People and Teams to Greatness: The Leading People & Teams to Greatness Course is not a traditional leadership training. It has been designed with one goal in mind—to help leaders lead their people and organisations to greatness. It is a highly intensive and interactive programme designed to help middle management staff/managers understand the challenges of today’s business environment, but more importantly, empower them with actionable concepts (through case studies, videos, role plays and instructor-led teaching), and practical strategies to lead themselves and their people to achieve greatness.

Duration: Three to Four Days

High I.M.P.A.C.T Leadership: This course has been designed for supervisors to help them learn how to lead their people through understanding the visioning process, setting direction, effective delegation and coaching, managing performance and holding people accountable for results, conducting performance appraise objectively, etc.

Duration: Three Days

Emotional Intelligence: According to Daniel Gole, author of the bestselling book Emotional Intelligence, emotional intelligence provides the underpinnings of collaboration, acceptance, and motivation – essentially, the core intangibles that cultivate a winning workforce. Emotional intelligence is about how people realize their potential and spur each other to greater achievements. It is about understanding the fundamentals of personality and human interaction to develop star performers and high-performing business groups and teams. Participants learn apply the concept of emotional intelligence to build healthy collaborative workplace and relationships that consistently deliver on business results.

Duration: 2 Days

Team Effectiveness Retreats/Programmes: improving team dynamics and chemistry to achieve business objectives.

Duration: 1 – 2 Days

Personal Effectiveness Masterclass: learn the dynamics of managing self, time, workload and others to achieve organisational outcomes.
Duration: 2 Days

Ideation & Strategy—Consulting Services

Strategy Formulation: clarify strategic direction, define the strategic objective of the organisation, make strategic decisions and allocate resources.

Strategic Innovation: unleash the creative energies of your workforce and disrupt the market place.

Core Competency Mapping: develop a competency framework, identify the core competencies of the organisation and business opportunities to pursue.

Growth Strategy: manage your business to boost and increase organic growth.

Culture & Execution

Culture Change & Alignment: conduct culture audit, identify gaps, make recommendations about new culture process and adoption.

Organisational Change & Business Transformation: use Strategy House’s 5-I model to create a structured approach to lead successful change and business transformation.

Performance Improvement: establish unique/outstanding systems for analysing performance issues/challenges and enhance performance and productivity.

Operation Process Improvement: identify gaps and improve business processes to execute your strategic objectives and meet/exceed your business goals.
